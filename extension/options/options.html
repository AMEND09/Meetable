Meeting Intelligence

Meetable automatically detects intent in captions to help you follow the meeting.

Question Detection
Captions ending with "?" are highlighted with a blue indicator.
Action Item Detection
Phrases like "we should", "action item", "I will" are flagged with a green indicator.
Moment Marking
Press Alt+M to bookmark important moments for later review.
Overlap Logging
Moments where captions may have been missed due to overlap are automatically logged.
Privacy

All speech recognition happens on-device using the browser's built-in Web Speech API. No audio is ever sent to external servers.

On-Device Processing Always enabled — speech data never leaves your device

Transcripts are stored locally in browser storage and are never uploaded.

Keyboard Shortcuts
Type a key combination like Alt+P or Shift+Alt+C. Changes take effect immediately.
